{% trans "About StudySphere" %}

StudySphere is a collaborative learning platform designed to support students who need additional academic guidance, skill development, or career direction.

The platform connects learners with educational resources, peer support, and structured study tools that help improve understanding and confidence across different subjects.

Our goal is to create an inclusive learning community where students can ask questions, share knowledge, discover learning opportunities, and explore possible career paths.

StudySphere encourages collaboration, respectful discussion, and continuous learning. Members can participate in study discussions, share educational resources, follow learning topics, and support others in their academic journey.

Whether you are catching up on challenging topics, developing new skills, or preparing for future opportunities, StudySphere provides a place to learn, grow, and connect with others who share similar goals.

Privacy Policy

Data & Privacy

Control whether your activity is visible on the platform. For how we handle your data, see this Privacy Policy.

StudySphere follows EU data protection and privacy law. Your data is used only to run the platform and to provide the services you use.

In line with that:

  • We do not collect additional information beyond what is needed for your account and your use of courses and features.
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties for their marketing or other purposes.
  • Your information is not used for advertising profiling or passed to advertisers.
  • Your activity on the platform is used only to provide the services you use (such as feeds and learning features), not for marketing or third-party purposes.

For questions about your data or to request a copy, contact us.
